Career Point University campus is spread surrounded by lower Himalayas in Hamirpur district in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. We provide a range of programs/ courses in Engineering and Technology, Computer Application and Information Technology, Sciences, Business Management, Legal Studies. Pharmaceutical & Health Sciences, Ayurveda, Hotel Management /Hospitality, Journalism, Tour & Travel Management, Digital Marketing Management Microbiology/Medical Microbiology, Arts, Commerce, Yoga , Naturopathy, Artificial intelligence, Data Science, Physical Education, Language, etc. we follow a time-tested, internationally acclaimed IIT & IIM based education system.
